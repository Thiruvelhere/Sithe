## 4. What to look for in a Testimonial

A good testimonial has 3 parts:
1.  **The Problem**: "We needed a way to prove our AI wasn't hallucinating..."
2.  **The Solution**: "Zypher's SDK was a drop-in solution..."
3.  **The Result**: "Now we can audit every single prompt."

**Example of a Perfect Testimonial:**
> "Integrating Zypher took us less than 10 minutes. It's the only solution we found that cryptographically binds the prompt to the output, which is a game-changer for our compliance requirements."
